Certificate: the action or process of providing someone or something with an official document attesting to a status or level of achievement. Online to Offline paring: Online-to-offline certificate is a business strategy that draws potential customers from online channels to physical stores. Online-to-offline certificate, or O2O, identifies customers in the online space, such as through emails and internet advertising, and then uses a variety of tools and approaches to entice the customer to leave the online space. This type of strategy incorporates techniques used in online marketing with those used in brick-and-mortar marketing.
